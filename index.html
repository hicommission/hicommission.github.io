Welcome to Africa Self-Help Solutions

This is an nteractive web-based platform for youth in our world to interact with others from their hometown in the diaspora, also for direct mentoring and coaching provided to the youth by professionals and volunteers who've excelled in diverse fields of praxis and then a platform where internship and scholarship and training / job opportunities can be announced and targeted to benefit youth from home.

Our Mission

Africa Self-Help is a non-profit organization that focuses on providing growth opportunities and expanding science, Innovation and Technology interlinkages for in and out-of-school youth in Blekusu, Volta Region, connecting them with information about interesting and exciting trends and initiatives in Ghana and around the world to create a happier and more fulfilling growth and development experience. We hope that our generation can create a sense of self-worth and confidence in our youth and pass a beautiful Ghana on to future generations. Projects

What would happen if we brought broadband internet to all the people in Blekusu and the neighboring towns?

Clearly, the rapid rollout of broadband services has transformed the lives of people all over the world. It should be no different for the people of our homeland. There is every reason to expect that everyone would benefit. It would enable many new enterprises to start and take root and all the spillovers that come with modern innovation – information, knowledge and education in mathematics, English and science will flourish, there will be more jobs, greater efficiency, bigger markets for our goods and services, and faster innovation. Tourism would get a major boost. It would also assist in achieving better outcomes in health and poverty reduction. In short, it would deliver a significant boost to human development and our local economy – raising our self-worth and our self-esteem.
