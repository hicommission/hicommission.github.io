VideoOrganizer – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11-16-2025

VideoOrganizer (“the App”) is designed to help you organize, browse, and manage your YouTube playlists more efficiently. We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and the choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Google Account Information (With Your Permission)

If you choose to sign in with your Google account, the App receives:

  • Your Google account identifier (non-sensitive)
  • YouTube playlist metadata (playlist name, ID, item count, thumbnails)
  • Public video metadata needed to display your playlists

We do NOT access, collect, or store:

  • Your Google password
  • Your private videos
  • Your comments, messages, or contact lists
  • Any sensitive personal data

1.2 App Usage Data

We may collect basic usage information such as:

  • App features used
  • Performance logs
  • Error/debug data

This information is anonymous and used only to improve the app’s reliability and functionality.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the data we access only to:

  • Display your YouTube playlists inside the App
  • Allow you to browse, sort, and filter your playlists
  • Improve app stability and user experience

We do not use your information for:

  • Advertising
  • Tracking
  • Profiling
  • Selling data to third parties

3. Data Storage & Security

  • No personal data is stored on our servers.
  • Playlist and account data retrieved from Google APIs is kept only on your device.
  • All communication with Google services uses secure, encrypted connections (HTTPS).

4. Third-Party Services

VideoOrganizer uses:

  • Google Sign-In to authenticate your account
  • YouTube Data API to access your playlists

Your use of these services is subject to Google’s Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

We do not share your data with any other third parties.

5. Your Choices

5.1 Disconnect Google Account

You may sign out at any time. When you sign out:

  • All playlist data is removed from your device
  • The App no longer accesses your Google account

5.2 Delete the App

Deleting the App permanently removes all locally stored data.

6. Children’s Privacy

VideoOrganizer is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. The updated version will be posted in the App and/or on our website.
